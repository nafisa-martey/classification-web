THE PROJECT

Our project is designed to detect whether or not foods are spoiled or unsafe for human consumption. According to Forbes, 40% of total food waste is made up of imperfect food — food that doesn't meet the strict cosmetic standards of grocery stores. We’ve found labeled images of safe and spoiled fruits through online research. Using this dataset, we trained a model that is able to identify whether or not an item of food is safe to eat or not. The user inputs a picture of fruit(s), and our program outputs a judgment over its safety. Our goal is to help users reduce their food waste by informing them on whether or not a food is truly safe to eat or not before throwing it out. This way user’s can make mindful decisions around the food they throw away.
